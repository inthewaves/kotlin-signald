Responding to a server's rate limit challenge that includes CAPTCHA as one of the options for the challenge.

The captchaToken should be obtained from Signal-Android's RECAPTCHA_PROOF_URL BuildConfig field in app/build.gradle (usually https://signalcaptchas.org/challenge/generate.html) and then completing a reCAPTCHA there.
